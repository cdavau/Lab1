BMW M3 GTR

Built for racetracks, master of the road" - for 20 years the 3 series has been entirely devoted to the world of motorsport. The fourth generation BMW M3 GTR is all about adding the finishing touches to perfection. A light, high revving naturally aspired V8 race engine sits at the front of the M3 GTR. With a peak power of 550 bhp at 8500 rpm and a 8700 rpm redline the M Power engine performs best when it is spinning fast. A distinctive carbon-fibre reinforced plastic roof helps reduce weight and significantly lower the centre of gravity while bonnet vents, rear spoiler and diffuser effectively minimize uplift. Motorsports is the DNA of the BMW M3 GTR.

Porsche Carrera GT

Originating from the pits of Le Mans after a 16th overall victory, the engineers went on to develop a concept to show what was technologically feasible. 1270 handcrafted units were built because exclusivity occasionally needs a limit. Underpinning the Carrera GT is a self-supporting carbon-fibre body which can handle 603 bhp and a speed of 201 mph while remaining incredibly light. The Carrera GT is lighter than the current lightest Porsche available.The world's best-selling high-performance car of 2006 is instantly recognisable as a Porsche.
